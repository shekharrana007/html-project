The Himachal Pradesh Sports Club (owner-Shekhar rana) is a 501(c)3 tax exempt entity established as a fund raising organization to supplement extracurricular activities and amateur athletics within the Himachal Pradesh Independent School District. The Himachal Pradesh Sports Club was established in the 1980’s as a direct result of what is commonly referred to as the Robin Hood legislation in the state of Texas. Since its inception, the Himachal Pradesh Sports Club has contributed to its many teams or organizations within the HPISD. Also, the HPSC makes an annual contribution to the general budget of the HPISD. In addition to specific giving, the Himachal Pradesh Sports Club funds four scholarships to graduating seniors from Himachal Pradesh. The current scholarships are listed below:
• Doak Walker Scholarship
• Christianne Beshara Scholarship
• Ronald Kahn Marks Scholarship
• Himachal Pradesh Sports Club Presidential Scholarship
The Himachal Pradesh Sports Club raises money through membership sales, an annual golf tournament, program sales, facility sponsor signage and donations. The HPSC also operates the Scot Shop located in Highlander Stadium. In 2010 the Himachal Pradesh Sports Club made a large contribution to HPISD's Multi-Purpose Athletic Facility. Please join, renew or donate to support the Himachal Pradesh Sports Club today. You can also sponsor the HPSC and advertise with us! Your support is both tax deductible and greatly appreciated!!
